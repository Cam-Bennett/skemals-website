About Camden

I didn't figure it out. I built my way out.

I know what it's like to end a day exhausted with nothing to show for it. Not because I didn't work. I worked constantly. I was available to everyone, responsive to everything, and in control of nothing.

I realized this when I took over as broker of Pinedale Properties, the top-producing real estate brokerage in Sublette County, Wyoming. More responsibility. More people depending on me. And a growing gap between how hard I was working and what I was actually producing.

I didn't have a discipline problem. I simply had no systems.
